LONG GRABBIT
OLIVE DRAB
The Long Grabbit tarp tie down is our 20" model. It is ideal for securing large spans of material subject to wind forces when suspended in the open such as with overhead cover. It is also ideal for large "flanking" spans of material such as tarps over a haystack, etc.
MINI GRABBIT
CANARY YELLOW
The Mini Grabbit tarp tie down is a miniature version of EZ Grabbit. At a mere 1.625" (40mm) in length its mini size has a mighty hold. It is ideal for lightweight tarp and tent shelters and is intended for recreational users who travel lightly.
