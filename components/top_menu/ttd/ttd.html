Getting around in this region
You're never more than two selections away from everything in this region.

Top menu: General Info and product offerings
Thumbnail menu: Specific product information.
Navigating the thumbnail section: Start by selecting a lettered button. The corresponding thumbnail images will show. Select a thumbnail to open its contents in the main view. Browse the main view as desired.

The INFO ABOUT THIS button: The INFO ABOUT THIS button provides info and links (as needed) regarding the current subject showing.

The LOCATION MENU button: The LOCATION MENU button often appears in any given view. Select it to find your locale and other locales.
History Section

Clear button: The "Clear" button clears your thumbnail history. Otherwise select a thumbnail history button to go back to a place you were before. When navigating, for example: selecting button B and selecting the third thumbnail will create thumbnail history button B-3.
